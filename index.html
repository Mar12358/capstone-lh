Main Dance Styles


  • Lindy Hop

    Wide range of tempos. Smooth and gentle, sometimes full of energy and crazy! (Partner)

  • Charleston

    Energic, large and crazy moves, it gets more challenging as tempo speeds up! (Partner)

  • Balboa

    Designed to use the minimum space possible and for faster tempos! (Partner)

  • Shag

    Quick, flashy footwork and a chugging rhythm. Very athletic and energic dance! (Partner)

  • Authentic Jazz

    From very slow to as fast as you can. The cradle of everything. (Solo)
